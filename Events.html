Events and activities

The events at this campsite will take place during holidays and seasons. The theme of the event will be based on the season or holiday. For example, if it was Fall there would be Fall themed activities and food. For the 4th of July there would be a cookout and a fireworks show.. Some of the activities are listed below.

Deep sea fishing

Kite flying

Drinking bars for adults

Volleyball

Dolphin sighting

Scuba Diving

Parasailing

Jet ski rides

Treasure hunting

Surfing
