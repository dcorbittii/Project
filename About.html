About page

Welcome to the about page. I am so excited to have you visit our campsite webpage. On this page, we will be advertising our activities and events. Our campsite is located on the North shore of the island of O'ahu, Hawaii. Here you will be able to enjoy a variety of activities and coastal scenery.

While at this campsite you will be able to enjoy a variety of activities. These activities include Deep sea fishing, Kite flying, Drinking bars for adults, Volleyball, Dolphin sighting, Scuba Diving, Parasailing, Jet ski rides, and Surfing. For our deep sea fishing activity, you will take one of our provided fishing boats 25 miles offshore from the campsite. For our scuba diving activity, campers will also be taken out on a boat a couple of miles offshore to explore the coral reefs. For our jet ski rides, we will have train employees take you for a ride across the coast and over the waves.

For sleeping areas, we will provide lodges for our campers. These lodges will come with a private bathroom per lodge with the choice of an outdoor or indoor shower. Some of these lodges provide 2 or 1 king-sized bed depending on the number of people and, specific lodges built for school trips are equipped with bunk beds. During the night hours, there will be bars for the adults to enjoy offering unlimited nonalcoholic and alcoholic drinks. Some of these drinks include popular Hawaiian alcholic drinks such as the Mai Tai, Blue Hawaii, Hawaiian Margarita, Lava Flow, Mango Martini.
